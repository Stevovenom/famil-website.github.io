About Us

about us

Faith, hope, and love in action every day

We are a vibrant community of believers dedicated to worship, fellowship, and service. Our mission is to share God's love, grow in faith, and make a positive impact in the world through compassionate outreach and meaningful connections.

Our church is a welcoming place where everyone can find support, inspiration, and a sense of belonging. Together, we strive to live out our faith and make a difference.

OUR CORE BELIEFS

Grounded in Biblical Truth

  • ✓ Holy Trinity & Biblical Authority — We believe in God the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, and the Bible as God's infallible word.
  • ✓ Salvation Through Christ — New birth and eternal life come through faith in Jesus Christ and His atoning blood.
  • ✓ Holy Spirit Empowerment — The Holy Spirit indwells believers and empowers us with spiritual gifts for ministry.
  • ✓ Living in Holiness — Character and conduct reflecting Christ's image supersede works alone.
