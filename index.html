about us

To be a church with a difference manifesting Christ's image

Family Fellowship Church of Christ is a vibrant Pentecostal community dedicated to the Holy Trinity. Since 2002, we've grown from a small assembly in Nairobi to a thriving network with branches across Kenya, serving over 748 members with diverse gifts and backgrounds.

We are a welcoming church where people of all tribes, races, and backgrounds find spiritual growth, compassionate care, and meaningful purpose. Together, we share God's love through worship, discipleship, and community service.

our mission

We exist to exalt Jesus Christ
